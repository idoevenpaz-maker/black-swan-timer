BLACK SWAN
Random Interval Timer
HOW IT WORKS
⏱️
TOTAL DURATION
Set how long your entire workout lasts, in minutes or seconds.
🎯
INTERVALS
Choose how many intervals you want. The app randomly decides when each one starts and how long it lasts — so you never know what's coming next.
📏
MIN / MAX DURATION
Control the shortest and longest an interval can be. Minimum is always 10 seconds. There's always at least 10 seconds of rest between intervals.
🔊
AUDIO CUES
5 beeps count down before each interval starts and ends. A sound plays when an interval begins and when it ends — perfect for earphones.
🟢
STATUS COLORS
Green = interval active  ·  Blue = rest period  ·  Orange = paused
💾
SAVE WORKOUT
Tap Save Workout to store your current settings under a name. Your saved workouts appear below — tap any one to instantly load its settings, or tap ✕ to delete it.
💡
PRO TIP
Put your phone in your pocket and just listen through your earphones — the audio cues tell you everything you need to know.
